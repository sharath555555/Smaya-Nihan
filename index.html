A Story I Wrote: The Little Cloud's Big Dream

This is a story about a tiny cloud who wanted to be the biggest, fluffiest cloud in the sky! Read along to see if her dream comes true.

The Little Cloud's Big Dream

Once there was a little cloud, no bigger than a kitten's sneeze. She watched the big, fluffy clouds drift by, carrying rain and sunshine to the world. "Oh," sighed the little cloud, "I wish I could be big and fluffy like them!"

The wind heard her wish. "Little cloud," whispered the wind, "to grow big, you must travel and collect tiny water droplets." So, the little cloud bravely drifted over oceans vast and mountains tall, collecting more and more tiny droplets. She grew bigger and bigger, until she was the fluffiest cloud in the sky! And she learned that even the smallest dream can come true with a little bit of adventure.

Amazing Science Fact: Why Is the Sky Blue?

Did you know the sky is blue because of tiny particles that scatter sunlight? Learn more cool facts!

Why Is the Sky Blue?

The sky is blue because of something called "Rayleigh scattering!" When sunlight comes to Earth, it hits tiny air particles. Blue light scatters more than other colors, so we see it all over the sky!

At sunset, the sun's light travels through more atmosphere, scattering out most of the blue light, leaving behind red and orange colors!
